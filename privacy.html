Privacy Policy for LoftTracks

Effective Date: October 7, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to LoftTracks ("the Application"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information. Our application operates entirely on your local machine. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data.

2. Information Handled by the Application

  • API Authentication Tokens: To interact with Google and Spotify APIs, the Application stores authentication tokens locally on your device. These are never sent to us.
  • User-Provided Data: Spotify URLs or local CSV files you provide remain on your computer.
  • Downloaded Files: Audio files are stored directly on your local hard drive.

3. How Information Is Used

Your information is used exclusively to authenticate with Spotify and Google APIs and to download the requested audio files to your local machine.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data. The only external communication is with the official APIs of Google and Spotify.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us. LoftTracks is operated from New York, USA.
